Questions answered

Everything you need to know.

```
Is Oronika Alliance really free? What's the catch? ▾
There is no catch. Oronika Alliance is a voluntary social initiative — not a business. Every program is 100% free for women and girls who need support. We sustain operations through voluntary donations and community partnerships. Your dignity is not a product we sell.
Is my privacy protected if I reach out for help? ▾
Absolutely. We take confidentiality seriously. All counseling sessions and support conversations are private. We never share personal information with third parties. Your safety is our entire reason for existing — we would never compromise it.
I'm not in crisis — can I still benefit from your programs? ▾
Yes! Our programs are designed for all women and girls — not just those in crisis. Skill development workshops, digital literacy training, and education awareness sessions are open to everyone. Empowerment doesn't wait for a breaking point.
How can I volunteer or help if I'm not a woman in need? ▾
We welcome volunteers with backgrounds in law, mental health, cybersecurity, education, and community organizing. You can also donate, amplify our message on social media, or connect us with organizations in your network. Every action builds the protection circle.
What if I'm in immediate danger? ▾
If you are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services first. Oronika Alliance can provide ongoing support, legal guidance, and counseling — but your immediate safety must always come first. Reach out to us after you are safe, and we will be here.
```
